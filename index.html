Principal's Message

👨‍🏫
Md. Abdur Rahman
Principal
Anushilan Pre-Cadet School
"Education is not merely the acquisition of knowledge — it is the formation of character, discipline, and vision."

Welcome to Anushilan Pre-Cadet School, where we believe every child carries the potential to become a leader. Our institution is dedicated to fostering academic excellence alongside the values of discipline, integrity, and service to the nation. Inspired by the cadet tradition, we train our students not only to excel in their studies but to carry themselves with confidence and purpose in all walks of life.

Why Choose Anushilan?

⚔️
Pre-Cadet Training
Unique cadet programme that builds discipline, leadership, and teamwork — preparing students for military academy entrance and beyond.
📖
Academic Excellence
Consistent 100% pass rates in SSC, with top GPA achievers every year. Our rigorous curriculum follows national board standards.
🏅
Award-Winning Faculty
Our experienced teachers bring passion and expertise to every class, guiding students with individual attention and care.
🏟️
Modern Campus
Science labs, a well-stocked library, large parade grounds, sports facilities, and comfortable classrooms create an ideal learning environment.
🤝
Holistic Development
Sports, cultural events, clubs, and community service ensure every student grows beyond academics into a well-rounded individual.
🌟
Strong Alumni Network
Our graduates go on to military academies, top universities, and influential careers across Bangladesh and abroad.
